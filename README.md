## About Winx - The Greatest Thoroughbred Racehorse

Winx is an Australian thoroughbred racehorse that many consider to be the greatest racehorse of all time. Born on September 14, 2011, this remarkable mare achieved an unprecedented level of dominance in horse racing.

### Career Highlights

- **Winning Streak**: Winx achieved an extraordinary 33-race winning streak from 2015 to 2019, which is the longest winning streak in modern international Group/Grade One racing
- **Major Victories**: She won four consecutive Cox Plates (2015-2018), Australia's weight-for-age championship race, a feat never accomplished by any other horse
- **Prize Money**: Winx earned over AUD $26 million in prize money, making her the highest-earning Australian racehorse in history
- **Racing Record**: Her career record was 43 starts, with 37 wins, including 25 Group One victories

### The Argument for Greatest of All Time

Several factors support the argument that Winx is the greatest thoroughbred racehorse of all time:

1. **Unprecedented Consistency**: Her 33-race winning streak at the highest level demonstrates remarkable consistency and dominance over an extended period
2. **Longevity at Peak Performance**: She maintained her peak form across multiple seasons, from ages 4 to 7, competing against the best horses in Australia and internationally
3. **Weight-for-Age Excellence**: Her four Cox Plate victories showcased her ability to compete at weight-for-age conditions, considered the truest test of thoroughbred excellence
4. **Versatility**: Winx demonstrated versatility by winning at various distances from 1400m to 2200m
5. **International Recognition**: She received numerous awards including the Longines World's Best Racehorse award in 2017 and 2018

### Legacy

Winx retired in 2019 and has become an icon of Australian sport. Her impact on racing extends beyond her victories, inspiring a new generation of racing fans and setting a standard of excellence that may never be matched.

While debates about the "greatest ever" are inherently subjective and complicated by different eras, competition levels, and racing conditions, Winx's achievements place her among the elite in thoroughbred racing history.
